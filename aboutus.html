What is ERP Software Implementation?

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software implementation refers to the process of introducing and integrating an ERP system into an organization's existing business processes. ERP is a type of software that provides a comprehensive suite of integrated applications designed to streamline and automate various business functions across different departments.

The ERP system typically includes modules for areas such as finance, human resources, supply chain management, manufacturing, inventory management, customer relationship management (CRM), and more.
